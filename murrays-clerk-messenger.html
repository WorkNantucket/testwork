Inventory Messenger & Clerk

Updated 31 December 2021
Full Time Retail

Full-Time Position: Inventory Messenger & Clerk for Murray’s Toggery Shop

Summary: The inventory messenger and clerk position inventories and keeps records on all items in the Nantucket Reds Collection. The clerk must also retrieve items located in our on island warehouse to replenish in store stock. The clerk must receive, organize, and store incoming items for stock from additional retail vendors. The clerk may assist customers with in store purchases.

Essential Functions: Receive, organize, deliver, and restock items for in store merchandise as determined by daily inventory.

    -Must be able to lift and transfer a maximum of 30 lbs of shipped goods from warehouse to van to store location.
    -Communicate via phone and in person with store managers to ask and answer questions regarding inventory.
    -Receive, open, and process new items shipped to warehouse and/or main store location. Restock retrieved items onto in-store displays and shelves.
    -Report defective materials, merchandise or questionable conditions to department managers.
    -Responsible for keeping an adequate supply and inventory of Nantucket Red items and other merchandise.
    -Maintain the work area, van, and equipment in an organized and clean condition
    -Visit on-island warehouse to retrieve necessary stock in order to refill inventory and orders. Restock and refill in store inventory from in store storage as well.
    -Ability and willingness to assist customers on the sales floor.

Competency Requirements:

    -Organizational skills
    -Time management
    -Written and verbal communication proficiency
    -Valid driver’s license

Position Type/Expected Hours of Work:

    Full time, year round
    5-6 hours a day
    5 days a week
    Flexible pay options based on experience, starting at minimum $20/hr

Required Education and Experience:

    Minimum of high school education or GED
    Ability to read, speak, and write proficiently as well as follow verbal directions and complete simple math calculations for sales and exchanges
    Valid U.S. driver’s license
HOUSING AVAILABLE: 2 bedroom apartment for rent year round, including utilities
