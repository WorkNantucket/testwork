Year Round Food Service

We are looking to hire year-round employees to work in our busy sandwich shop and bakery! We are looking for applicants with some experience whether that’s while in school, internship or post graduation. An ideal employee would be looking to advance both their culinary and management skills.

Starting pay ranges from $21 - $27 per hour including tips and is based on experience. We offer Health Insurance, a pension plan, vacation pay, employee housing, employee meals, and a signing & year-end bonus.

We’re ideal for a young person who wants the security of a year-round job, but the opportunity to take time off and travel as we close the retail store in January and February. This allows staff a break to recharge for the next season.

Please email terry@somethingnatural.com your resume and for an application!
