Customer Service Agent

Updated on 1 January 2022
Part Time

Southern Airways Express is now hiring for Customer Service Agents in Nantucket!

Position Summary:

The Airport Customer Service Agent is our primary representative in our airport stations. The position is responsible for handling all customer service activities, including greeting and checking in passengers, handling luggage,
booking /re-booking flights, and assisting with passenger loading /unloading. Agents are responsible for all ramp duties.

Benefits:

    Competitive pay;
    Flexible hours;
    Comprehensive training included;
    No prior aviation experience required;
    Travel benefits for you and your travel companion on Southern Airways Express, Mokulele Airlines, and numerous major airlines;
    Discounts on hotels, rental cars, and cruises.

How to apply:
Apply online at www.iflysouthern.com under the careers tab.
For more information:
Inquire in person at the ticket counter OR
call 401-648-7621 (Ask for Brian)

Apply
On the website under "Careers".
