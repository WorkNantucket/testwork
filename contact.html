Job Listing Template

To submit a job for posting on WorkNantucket use any of the above listed contact methods.

  • Please include the following details in each submission:
  • Job Title
  • Responsibilities:
    Describe the job duties; Duration; full/part time, seasonal/year round, volunteer, intern, etc;
  • Who you're looking for:
    Describe your ideal candidate, qualifications required;
  • Benefits, wage/salary;
    Do you have housing available or can you help find it? Listings with no pay information will not be posted.
  • How to apply;
    direct email address or phone number, or link to application on your website.
  • Include company logo or picture, as png or jpg;
  • Links to Indeed, LinkedIn, Monster, etc not accepted. No recruiters.
  • Jobs will be posted to WorkNantucket in order of receipt.
