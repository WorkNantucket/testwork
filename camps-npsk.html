Nantucket Community School
Summer Positions

Updated 18 January 2022
Full Time Seasonal

  • Assistant Camp Director
  • Lead Camp Counselor
  • 1:1 Support Day Camp
  • Camp Counselor- Lifeguard certified and/or WSI certified
  • Gymnastics Head Coach

NCS is seeking individuals to fill the positions listed above. All positions are temporary/seasonal employment during the summer months. The pay range is between $18.00 and $35.00 per hour, depending upon the candidate's level of experience and the responsibilities of the job. Candidates will be expected to work Monday through Friday during the day, with potential for some evening and/or weekend hours depending upon the position and availability.

Recommended Minimum Qualifications

  • Applicants must be at least 16 years of age by 1 June 2022 to apply for paid positions
  • Current CPR and First Aid certification required by 1 June 2022
  • Positive attitude and high energy are a must
  • Experience working with children
  • Day Camp staff need to commit to entire camp season (Tentatively 27 June - 19 August)

Definition
Supervise, coach, and engage children ages 3 1/2 years - 14 years of age in a summer day camp/sports camp environment during the summer months.

Supervision
Work under the direct supervision of the Childcare Coordinator, Coach/Lead Counselor, Aquatics Director, and Nantucket Community School Director.

Job Environment
Programs are based out of Nantucket Public School's campus. Playing fields, pool, and gymnasiums are located at 10 Surfside Road. ACKventure Day Camp applicants will be expected to actively participate in daily/weekly field trips to beaches, historical sites, nature walks, playgrounds, sports fields, community pool, etc.

Application
Interested Candidates should submit a letter of interest and resume via email:
Shauna Carthy, carthys@npsk.org

These positions are open until filled.
