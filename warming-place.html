The Warming Place

Updated 6 March 2022
Volunteer Community Service

Call for volunteers!

The Warming Place is open 8:30am - 1:30pm on Mondays and Thursdays at the Summer Street Church.

Volunteers are needed for 30 minutes to pick up and deliver breakfast and lunch, and for 3 hours shifts to help with set-up, clean-up, and welcoming our guests.

No special skills required. Please bring proof of vaccination.

To view unfilled time slots, and sign up to volunteer, visit the SignUpGenius link: https://www.signupgenius.com/go/10c0f4ba9ab22a4f9c07-volunteer

Please review the available slots below and click on the button to sign up. The meal pick up and delivery slots should take no longer than 30 minutes, perfect for those who want to help but can't leave work for 3 hours. We are grateful for all the ways people contribute!

For questions, contact the ackhomelessnessteam via email or phone 774-236-8970
